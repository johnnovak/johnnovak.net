John Novak presents

Best of the Internet™

v0.1beta

An annotated collection of my favourite places on the web that live up to its original vision: free quality content and information for all

Music

AmbientMusicGuide.com
A guide to essential ambient & downtempo albums. The number one resource for discovering new ambient artists.
Kahvi Collective
One of the oldest and most prestigious ambient/electro/IDM netlabels in existence. Over 350+ quality releases, all free to download and enjoy, many of them by (ex-)demosceners.
Drone Zone
Droning atmospheric space music and ambient textures with minimal beats. Music on Drone Zone is all about sonic textures and environments.
Deep Space One
Deep ambient electronic, experimental and space music. Music for getting lost in space, or just exploring.
Space Station
Mostly analog-inspired mid- and downtempo electronic music, from 1990 to now. Broadcasting from geostationary orbit to listeners here on Earth and throughout the solar system.
musicForProgramming
A series of mixes intended for listening while programming to aid concentration and increase productivity (also compatible with other activities).
Philosomatika
Simply the best psytrance / progressive psytrance radio this side of the Galaxy.
Nectarine
Demoscene radio.
Remix.Kwed.Org [RKO]
The definitive guide to C64 remakes.

Coding

Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs [SICP]
The classic textbook "Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs" by Abelson, Sussman, and Sussman has never looked better! Now in HTML5/PDF/EPUB3.
Learn You a Haskell for Great Good!
One of the best introductions to general functional programming concepts—incidentally, it also does a fine job at teachnig you some basic and intermediate Haskell skills.
The MIDI Technical Fanatic's Brainwashing Center
The MIDI Technical Fanatic's Brainwashing Center is a cyberplace dedicated to those individuals who wish to attain a higher level of existence than that of the plodding, MIDI-illiterate scum around them.
The ryg blog
Tech blog of ryg/Farbrausch (Fabian Giesen) full of some extremely useful performance and graphics programming info.
Random ASCII
Tech blog of the author of CygnusED on the Amiga, focusing heavily on performance related stuff on Windows.
Nim
Nim (formerly known as "Nimrod") is a statically typed, imperative programming language that tries to give the programmer ultimate power without compromises on runtime efficiency.

Graphics

Scratchapixel 2.0
Excellent introduction to 3D computer graphics from the group up, with special emphasis on various raytracing techniques and physically-based rendering.
Íñigo Quílez
Personal homepage of Íñigo Quílez containing a greath wealth of articles and tutorials on generative art.
Learn OpenGL
The best learning materials for modern core-profile OpenGL programming.

Demoscene

pouët.net
The #1 demoscene portal on the planet.
Capped.TV
Video captures of demoscene productions for the masses and lazy sceners.
Amiga Demoscene Archive [ADA]
A fine selection of the best Amiga demos with screenshots and download links.

Amiga

Classic Workbench [ClassicWB]
Powerful yet minimal classic Workbench setups (1.3 & 3.0/3.1) for real Amigas and WinUAE.
